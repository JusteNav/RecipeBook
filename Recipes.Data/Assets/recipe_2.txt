Apple and Carrot Salad

INGREDIENTS
4 apples 
4 carrots
2 teaspoon sugar optional
2 tablespoons sour cream

INSTRUCTIONS
Step 1 Wash, peel and grate apples and carrots. Combine. 
Step 2 Add sugar and sour cream. Mix everything thoroughly so that the sugar melts to the maximum and serve the delicious salad to the table.

NOTES
If you won't immediately consume the salad, spray with a little bit of lemon juice and leave in the fridge until ready. Consume the same day though.
