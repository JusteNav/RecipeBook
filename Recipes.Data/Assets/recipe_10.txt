
Milk Soup with Noodles

INGREDIENTS
1 l milk (3.5%)
250 g pasta (Noodles)
50 g sugar
1 pinch salt (tip of a teaspoon)


INSTRUCTIONS
Step 1  Dip the pasta (Noodles) in boiling salted water and cook according to the instructions on the package until almost cooked (4-6). Drain the finished pasta.
Step 2  Boil the milk separately over low heat (so that the milk does not burn, you can first pour some cold water on the bottom of the boiling).
Step 3 Add pasta, sugar, salt in the boiled milk and cook for about 3-8 minutes (until the pasta is cooked) over low heat.
