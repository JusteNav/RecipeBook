          

Light Crab Salad Recipe

INGREDIENTS
2 cups (250g) crab meat, sliced
1 paprika, sliced
1/2 red onion, sliced
2 small cucumbers, sliced
1 1/2 cups cauliflower, small florets
salt & pepper to taste
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon dill

INSTRUCTIONS
Step 1  Slice crab, pepper, cucumbers, onion into thin slices. Cut cauliflower in small florets.
Step 2  Combine all ingredients together.
Step 3 Add dill, add mayo, salt and pepper. Mix everything.
Step 4 Add some more dill for decor if desired. Serve.
