Ham and Cheese Pannini

INGREDIENTS
2 slices of ham,
2 slices of cheese, 
2 slices of Bread, toasted medium,

Microwave safe plate

INSTRUCTIONS
Step 1 Make the sandwich on the plate.
Step 2  Toast Bread.
Step 3  Put the toast on the plate.
Step 4  Put cheese on hot toast.
Step 5  Put ham in the middle.
Step 6  Put top on sandwich.
Step 7  Slam it with your hand and smash it altogether.
Step 8  Microwave 15 - 30 seconds (Not longer, it will get yucky.)
